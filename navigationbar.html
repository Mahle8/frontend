About Us

Like tiny wind chimes our jewelry are are a cast in silver, cold to the touch yet warm on skin. Our range of products comes from the purest raw minerals embarked with scent. Mcebo creations deals with a range of jewelry , from the wire work jewelry to Kundan jewelry and designing of own jewelry

OUR PRODUCTS

ANTIQUE JEWELRY

Antique jewelry is sometimes expressed as vintage jewelry the difference being the time period. jewellery is considered antique when its 100 years old while vintage if its 50 years old.

KUNDAN JEWELRY

Kundan in Sanskrit means ‘pure gold’. our Kundan jewelry is a style of setting gems usually uncut diamond or polks using carat gold foil.

MINAKARI JEWELRY

This is inspired by the idian art of enamelling metal with vibrant colours and beautiful designs
